### Entity Mapping Model
In Composio, an **Entity** represents a user or organization in your system.
* **1 CRM Client in Hyphening = 1 Composio Entity**
  * `Entity ID` format: `hyphening_client_<client_id>` (e.g., `hyphening_client_12`).
  * Under this single Entity ID, a client can connect all their brand social channels:
    * `instagram` (Instagram Business Reels, Posts, Stories & Insights)
    * `youtube` (YouTube Shorts, Long-form Video Uploads & Analytics)
    * `linkedin` (LinkedIn Company Pages & Personal Video Posts)
    * `facebook` (Facebook Page Posts & Video Reels)
    * `x` (Twitter Video Posts & Tweets)
    * `google_ads` / `meta_ads` (Ad Campaign Reporting & ROAS Tracking)

## 3. High-Quality Direct Auto-Publishing (No Meta Suite Compression)

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Why Video Quality is Preserved**:
> Uploading videos via Meta Business Suite in a browser causes Meta's web client to aggressively re-encode, compress, and lower the bitrate of your video on your computer before uploading.
> Direct API uploads via Composio stream the **uncompressed original video file** from Google Drive directly to Meta & YouTube server ingestion pipelines, preserving full 1080p 60fps quality and color fidelity.

## 4. OpenClaw Telegram Co-Pilot & Script Tracker Workflows

1. **Telegram Command-to-Publish**:
   * Text OpenClaw on Telegram: *"Post the reel from this Drive link to Client X's Instagram tomorrow at 6 PM for Script #14."*
   * OpenClaw generates the caption, validates the Drive link, sets `scheduled_at`, links `script_id`, and presents a Telegram Inline Confirmation button: **`[Confirm & Schedule]`**.

2. **Guided 2-Step Telegram Selector (Client & Script)**:
   * **Step A — Interactive Client Selector (If Client Not Specified)**:
     * If the user sends a Drive link without specifying the client name, OpenClaw queries active clients via deterministic SQL: `SELECT id, name FROM crm_clients WHERE is_active = 1`.
     * Renders clickable client buttons in Telegram:
       * 🔘 **Client 1: Dentalchemy**
       * 🔘 **Client 2: D2C Brand Co.**
       * 🔘 **Client 3: Hyphening Media**
   * **Step B — Zero-Hallucination Month-Filtered Script Selector**:
     * Once the client is selected, OpenClaw runs a deterministic query for that client's target month: `SELECT id, title FROM marketing_scripts WHERE client_id = ? AND month = '2026-07'`.
     * Renders the exact script titles as clickable buttons:
       * 🔘 **Reel 1: "5 Common Myths About Teeth Whitening Busted"**
       * 🔘 **Reel 2: "The Right Way to Floss (Step-by-Step Guide)"**
       * 🔘 **Reel 3: "Signs You Might Need a Root Canal"**
       * 🔘 **⏩ [Skip Script Link]**
   * **Strict Callback Binding**: Tapping a script button binds `client_id` and `script_id` directly in the database (`marketing_content_script_relation`), guaranteeing 100% data integrity with zero hallucination risk!

3. **Closed-Loop Script Performance Analytics**:
   * OpenClaw uses Composio's `INSTAGRAM_GET_USER_MEDIA` and `YOUTUBE_GET_VIDEO_STATS` to track retention and views.
   * Reports back to Telegram which written scripts and hook styles produced the highest views and conversions.

1. **`composioService.js`**:
   * Wraps `@composio/core` SDK.
   * Provides `getConnectUrl(clientId, appName)` and `executeClientAction(clientId, actionName, params)`.

2. **`autoPublisher.js`**:
   * Cron worker checking `marketing_content_tracker WHERE status = 'Scheduled' AND scheduled_at <= datetime('now')`.
   * Streams video file from Google Drive URL to Composio API for direct, uncompressed publishing.
   * Auto-links `script_id` and updates status to `Posted`.

3. **`dailyCommentSync.js`**:
   * Cron worker running **once per day** (2:00 AM UTC) to pull recent comments across Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts via Composio.
   * Caches comments locally in `social_comments` table to preserve API limits (2,000–10,000 requests/min capacity) and avoid excessive background polling.

4. **`metricSyncWorker.js` (Bi-Weekly 2-Stage Metric Refresh)**:
   * **2-Stage Refresh Schedule**:
     * **Stage 1 (Day 7)**: Syncs metrics 7 days post-publish to capture viral velocity.
     * **Stage 2 (Day 30)**: Syncs metrics 30 days post-publish to lock in final lifetime performance.
     * **On-Demand**: Manual **`[ 🔄 Sync Live Metrics ]`** button available on any Reel row in Content Tracker.
   * **Dual ID Reference Architecture**:
     * Internal Reference: `content_id` (Primary Key in `marketing_content_tracker`).
     * Platform Reference: `instagram_media_id` / `youtube_video_id` / `linkedin_post_id` returned by Composio.
     * The worker queries `instagram_media_id` via Composio, then updates the row matching `content_id`.

## 5. Direct High-Bitrate Video Auto-Publishing & Free Tier Quota Architecture

### A. High-Quality Direct Video Auto-Publishing Scope
Auto-publishing is focused **100% on direct video content** (Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts & Videos, LinkedIn Videos, Facebook Reels, and X Videos).

1. **Original Quality Streaming**:
   * Videos are streamed directly from Google Drive to platform ingestion servers via Composio.
   * **Bypasses Meta Web Re-encoding**: Preserves full 1080p 60fps quality, color profile, and original high audio bitrates.
2. **Carousel Scope**:
   * Carousels will be posted manually by the user directly in the Instagram app to leverage native Instagram app features (e.g. music library, interactive stickers).
   * Auto-publishing pipeline processes only scheduled video content.

### B. Managed Quota & Rate Limit Control (20,000 Free Tool Calls/Month)

Composio's Hobby (Free) Tier provides **20,000 Standard Tool Calls per month** (~666 calls/day total).

| Action Type | Frequency & Calculation | Monthly Composio Calls | % of Free Tier |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Video Auto-Publishing** | 1 video/day across 2 platforms = 2 calls/day * 10 clients * 30 days | **600 calls/mo** | 3.0% |
| **Metric Analytics Refresh** | 2-Stage sync (Day 7 & Day 30) = 60 syncs/client * 10 clients | **600 calls/mo** | 3.0% |
| **Daily Comment Ingestion** | 1 daily sync cron (2:00 AM UTC) * 10 clients * 30 days | **300 calls/mo** | 1.5% |
| **Comment Replies** | ~2 manual replies/day * 10 clients * 30 days | **600 calls/mo** | 3.0% |
| **OAuth Connects & Auth** | ~2 auth calls/month * 10 clients | **20 calls/mo** | 0.1% |
| **TOTAL ESTIMATED USAGE** | **10 Clients Fully Automated** | **2,120 / 20,000 calls** | **~10.6% Used** |

#### Managed Quota Guardrails (Zero Cost Overhead Guarantee):
1. **Quota Tracking Table (`sys_composio_quota_logs`)**:
   * Logs every API call made to Composio with timestamp and remaining monthly quota.
2. **Automated Alerting at 80% Threshold**:
   * If total calls in a billing cycle exceed 16,000 (80%), OpenClaw fires an alert to Telegram: *"⚠️ Composio Quota Alert: 80% free allowance used. Non-essential background analytics sync paused."*
3. **No Polling Loops & SQLite Caching**:
   * Scheduled posts check SQLite local state. All client portal views render from SQLite cache with **0 Composio calls**.
